Why is CSS important?

CSS is important because it controls all design-related aspects of a website. Typography, colours, page layouts and any other visual aspects of a website are all controlled by CSS. It allows you to control and adjust the design and formatting of your entire website from a single spot.

Padding: The padding is the space between the content and the border of a block, padding separates the border from the content.

Margin: The margin, on the other hand, is the space outside the border of a block. Margin separates blocks from adjacent blocks.

Border: Border is the strip that goes around your div/element of the content.
